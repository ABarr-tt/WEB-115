Welcome to Barrett's Coffee

We are a family-owned coffee shop dedicated to providing our customers with the best coffee experience possible. Our passion for coffee drives us to source the finest beans from around the world and craft them into delicious blends that you'll love.

Our Commitment

At Barrett's Coffee, we are committed to sustainability and community. We work directly with coffee farmers to ensure fair trade practices and support environmental initiatives. With each cup of coffee, you're not just supporting a local business, but also making a positive impact on the world.
